eVoCounter

A modern lifestyle calculator and progress tracker that determines a user's positive impact and growth as a result of using public transportation over personal auto use.

Inspired Change

Our eVoCounter makes sure you know the extent of your positive impact on the environment as well as the financial and health benefits that come with the regular use of public transport. Having an awareness of the benefits of smart transportation use can help turn your actions into habits, and habits into a rewarding lifestyle.

group

Interactive UI

By creating a "gamified" platform that makes daily user engagement with VIA fun and interactive, we hope to encourage the public to weaken their reliance on personal transportation and use public bus systems to get to where they need to be. For example, a rider can set goals to increase his or her bus ridership and witness the positive impacts on environment, personal health, and their wallet over time.

Easy To Use

We have developed eVoCounter to deliver a simple, user friendly interface that is able to be used by anybody, from kids to seniors. We want everyone to have the opportunity to see the positive changes they can make through responsible use of transportation, and we believe that eVoCounter can directly influence the way people see public transportation in the future.



Purpose Statement

VIAVistas was created to create better solutions to the problem of overcrowding in urban areas, and the pollution and infrastructure obstacles that come from it. We hope to inspire people to make positive and socially responsible choices in regards to their daily transportation needs. We plan to do this through innovation and the use of popular social channels to spread awareness of public transportation and the numerous benefits it provides for citizens.
